Wireless LED Uplighting About

Lighting is an elegant and creative way to make a big impact on your room’s decor . It adds warmth and ambiance to your space while highlighting the natural architecture. The end result is a beautiful look that’s elegant and classy for dinner, and during the dance set the color patterns add energy and excitement for a total party experience.

Our state-of-the-art wireless LED uplights are battery powered and can be placed anywhere based upon your venue’s unique layout. Since they are LED they never get hot to the touch. The color can be customized to match your event’s theme. We suggest one static color for cocktail hour and dinner, then change to moving color patterns during the dance set.
